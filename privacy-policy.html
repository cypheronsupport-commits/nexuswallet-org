NexusVPN Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

NexusVPN ("we", "our", "the extension") is a browser extension that routes your web traffic through proxy servers to protect your privacy and provide anonymous browsing. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

NexusVPN collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity.
  • We do not log your IP address or connection timestamps.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party analytics.

3. Data Stored Locally

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your selected server preference
  • VPN connection state (on/off)
  • Theme preference (light/dark)
  • VPN bypass domain list
  • Protection settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

4. Network Traffic

When the VPN is active, your browser traffic is routed through third-party proxy servers. We do not operate these proxy servers and do not have access to your traffic data. The proxy configuration is handled entirely through Chrome's built-in proxy API.

5. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Proxy: To configure Chrome's proxy settings and route traffic through VPN servers.
  • Storage: To save your preferences locally on your device.
  • WebRequest: To handle proxy authentication.
  • Alarms: To maintain connection status periodically.
  • Host permissions: To route all web traffic through the VPN when enabled.

6. Remote Code

NexusVPN does not load or execute any remote code. All extension code is bundled within the extension package.

7. Third-Party Services

NexusVPN does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties. No data is collected, therefore no data is shared.

9. Children's Privacy

NexusVPN does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
