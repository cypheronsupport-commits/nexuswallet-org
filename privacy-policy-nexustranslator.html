Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Introduction

Nexus Translator ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

Nexus Translator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity.
  • We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts.
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles.

Text Processing

When you use the translation feature, the selected text is sent directly to the MyMemory Translation API (mymemory.translated.net) to provide the translation. This is the only external service the extension communicates with for translation purposes.

When you use the Explain feature on a single word, the text may be sent to the Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) to retrieve definitions.

The Summarize and Rewrite features process text entirely locally in your browser. No data is sent to any external server for these features.

Data Storage

The extension stores only your language preferences (source and target language) locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

  • MyMemory Translation API — Used for text translation. Their privacy policy can be found at mymemory.translated.net.
  • Free Dictionary API — Used for word definitions. An open-source API with no data collection.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab / tabs — To detect text selection and display the toolbar and sidebar on the current page.
  • contextMenus — To add right-click menu options for quick access to tools.
  • storage — To save your language preferences locally.
  • scripting — To inject the sidebar interface into web pages.

Children's Privacy

The extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
