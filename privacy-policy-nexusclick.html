Nexus CLICK

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nexus Click ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Nexus Click operates entirely on your local device. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • We do not track your browsing history or visited websites
  • We do not record or transmit your mouse clicks or interactions
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools within the extension
  • We do not collect crash reports or usage statistics

2. Data Stored Locally

Nexus Click stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • User Settings: Click interval, click type, theme preference, replay speed, and other configuration options
  • Saved Recordings: Click sequences you choose to save, including element selectors, coordinates, and timing
  • UI Preferences: Popup/side panel mode selection

This data is stored only on your local device and is never transmitted to any external server. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome settings.

3. Permissions

Nexus Click requires the following browser permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To interact with the current webpage for auto-clicking and recording
  • tabs: To detect tab changes and identify restricted pages
  • storage: To save your settings and recordings locally
  • scripting: To inject click automation scripts into web pages
  • sidePanel: To display the extension interface in Chrome's side panel
  • All URLs (content script): To enable clicking and recording on any webpage you visit

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and are not used to collect or transmit any data.

4. Third-Party Services

Nexus Click displays advertisements provided by Google AdSense on an interstitial page hosted at nexuswallet.org. Google AdSense may use cookies and collect data according to Google's Privacy Policy. This advertising is served through a web page and is separate from the extension itself.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to share.

6. Children's Privacy

Nexus Click does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension does not require any personal information to function.

7. Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted over the internet by the extension. The extension operates entirely offline with the exception of the optional advertisement page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
